How we work

A clear path,
from first call
to closing.

No surprises. No pressure. Just a structured process built over 12 years, designed to protect you and get the deal done right.

01
First call
We talk. Really talk.

Not emails, not a contact form, a real conversation. We want to understand who you are, what you're looking for, your budget, and what matters most. You get a clear picture of who you're dealing with before anything else happens.

15–30 minutes · No obligation
02
Property search
You only see what's worth your time.

Based on what you've told us, we go to work. There is no MLS in Mexico, the market is fragmented, and not every developer or seller deserves your attention. We filter everything, reputation, delivery history, numbers, and send you only what genuinely fits, with our straight honest take on each one.

No inbox flooding. Only what's relevant.
03
Video call
We go through it together.

A video call to walk through the shortlist, screen-share properties, dig into the numbers, answer every question. If a partner or spouse is part of the decision, this is the moment to bring them in. By the end of this call, we know exactly what we're going after.

You arrive having already reviewed the selection we've sent.
04
Visit preparation
When you come, everything is ready.

About a week before you arrive, we go over your shortlist one more time, confirm availability, and schedule your viewings in half-days, enough to see everything without hitting information overload. You land knowing exactly what the plan is.

Can't make it in person? We can move forward remotely too.
05
Offer & deposit
When it's right, we move fast.

Good properties don't wait. When you've found the one, we negotiate on your behalf, price, terms, timeline, and guide you through securing it with a deposit. We've seen too many clients lose their favourite property by waiting a week too long.

We handle the negotiation. You make the final call.

The closing isn't the end of our relationship, it's the beginning of a longer one. We check in. We stay involved. Whether it's a question about your rental income, a renovation recommendation, or the right moment to sell, we're still there.

We built B&D Realty to work with people for years, not transactions. The clients we've had the longest are the ones who came back, and brought their friends.
