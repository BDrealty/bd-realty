The founders

Built by closers.
Designed for private clients.

Jennifer and I built B&D Realty after 12 years in the Riviera Maya, seeing the same problem again and again: plenty of agents could show property, but very few could truly read the deal — or stay close enough to the client when it mattered. My background is in portfolio management and high-conversion sales, with a focus on numbers, positioning, negotiation, and execution. Jennifer brings a background in event organization, where discretion, precision, timing, and client experience are non-negotiable.

Coming from the UK and France, we bring an international standard to a local market we know deeply. But more than anything, we built B&D Realty to be the kind of advisory the market was missing: present, honest, strategic, and fully invested in the outcome. For buyers, investors, and sellers who expect more than access to listings, our role is simple — bring clarity, protect the deal, and stand beside you from first conversation to closing table.
