The founders

We didn't start as agents.
We started as buyers.

We came here, bought, made the mistakes, and learned more from those than from anything else. Now we live here with two kids, two dogs, and no plans to leave.

That's what built B&D Realty, a genuine frustration with a market that wasn't putting the client first. You are the whole point.

My background is in portfolio management and high-conversion sales. I think in numbers, positioning, and outcomes. Jennifer comes from event production, a world where discretion, precision, and client experience are non-negotiable. Coming from the UK and France, we bring an international standard to a market we know deeply from the inside.

Every deal we take on, we treat as if it's our own money on the line. We know which developers keep their word and which ones don't. We know the deals that never make it online. And we know what it feels like to sit where you're sitting, which is exactly why we show up differently.
